ZNACKERS AFTERNOON EXOTIC FRUIT TASTING. ADULTS AND CHILDREN WELCOME (NYC)

DESCRIPTION
​Give your taste buds an adventure.  Come join Znackers Exotic Fruit Tasting where you can try the Dragon Fruit, Durian, Persimmon, Asian Pear, Lychee, Longan, Water Apple, Rambutan, Cherimoya, Mangosteen, Star Fruit, Guava, Prickly Pear, or other types of exotic fruits.  All of the fruits are delicious and nutritious.  Their true taste is indescribable, so you have to try it for yourself.
