About Us

​Znackers Snack Boxes are Perfect for Any Occasion
Perfect for dorms, offices, parties, gatherings, and road trips. It also makes a great gift and care package for someone you care about.

Let Us Save You Time and Enhance Productivity
Get a variety of snacks or instant noodles to choose from all in one box without traveling or waiting in line.  With our snack boxes you can increase your employee’s productivity by having a variety of snacks conveniently available to curve their work hunger.  Our snack boxes will help your employees make less trips to the snack store and spend more time working.
